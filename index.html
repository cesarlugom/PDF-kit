Common questions

Is PDF Kit a subscription?
No. PDF Kit is a one-time purchase from the Microsoft Store. Pay once and it's yours forever, including all future updates within the same major version.
Does PDF Kit upload my files anywhere?
Never. All processing happens locally on your PC. No cloud, no uploads, no account required. Your contracts, tax returns, and personal documents stay completely private.
Can I highlight text and see it in other PDF readers?
Yes. Highlights are saved as standard PDF annotations. They're visible in Microsoft Edge, Adobe Acrobat Reader, Chrome, and any other PDF viewer that supports the PDF standard.
What makes the compression different?
Most free tools only strip metadata or apply basic deflation. PDF Kit actually resamples images inside the PDF at the DPI you choose (72, 150, or 300). A 122 MB file becomes 27 MB — 78% smaller — with quality you can control.
Does it work offline?
Yes. PDF Kit works 100% offline. The only network call is Microsoft Store license verification, which is handled by Windows — not by our app.
